Add Funds to Steam Wallet

Add funds to your Steam Wallet to use it as an alternate payment option.

  1. In the top right corner, click your username.
  2. Click View my walletin the dropdown menu.
  3. Click Add Funds next to your preferred fund level.
  4. Click the dropdown and choose the payment method that you'd like to use:
    1. If using Paypal, click Continue and sign into your Paypal account.
    2. If using a credit card, fill in your credit card information then press Continue.
  5. Check the I agree to the terms of the Steam Subscriber Agreement check box.
  6. Click Purchase.
