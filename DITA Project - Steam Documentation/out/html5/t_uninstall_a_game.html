Uninstall a Game

If you no longer play a game and want to remove it from your computer, you can uninstall it.

  1. In the left panel, right-click the game you'd like to uninstall.
  2. Hover over Manage.
  3. Click Uninstall.
  4. Read the warning pop-up and, if you wish to continue, click Uninstall again. The process may take a few seconds to complete depending on the size of the game.
