Steam Library

Your Library page is the primary hub where you can access all your purchased games.

The Library page is where all the games you own live. This is where you can install, update, launch, and uninstall your games. You can also sort, filter, search, organize, and display your games in a manner of your liking. This is also where you can access news, the Steam Workshop, community pages, and technical support for specific games you own.

  • Manage Collections
    Collections let you organize games in a manner of your choosing.
  • Install a Game
    Before you can play your new Steam game, you have to install it onto your computer.
  • Hide a Game
    You can hide a game if you no longer want it to appear in your Library.
  • Manage Favorites
    Your favorites list can be used to prominently display games that you are particularly fond of.
  • Uninstall a Game
    If you no longer play a game and want to remove it from your computer, you can uninstall it.
  • Add a Non-Steam Game
    Even if you own a game on a platform other than Steam, you can still add it to your Steam Library.
  • Activate a Product on Steam
    Steam games that are bought from a third party seperate from the Steam Store must be manually activated using a code.
  • Sort and Filter Library
    You can make it easier to find certain types of games in your Library by sorting and filtering.
