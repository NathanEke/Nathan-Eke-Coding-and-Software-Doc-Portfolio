Buy a Game

Once you've found a game on the Steam Store that is to your liking, you can purshcase it and add it toy your Library.

  1. Navigate to the game's store page.
  2. Click Add to Cart.
  3. Click View my Cart.
  4. Click Continue to Payment.
  5. Click the dropdown and choose the payment method that you'd like to use.
    1. If using Paypal, click Continue and sign into your Paypal account.
    2. If using a credit card, fill in your credit card information then press Continue.
  6. Check the I agree to the terms of the Steam Subscriber Agreement check box.
  7. Click Purchase.
