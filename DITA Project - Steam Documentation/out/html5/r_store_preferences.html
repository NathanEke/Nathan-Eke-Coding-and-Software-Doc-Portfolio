Store Preferences

Is there certain content you don't want to see on your Steam Store page? Set your preferences so that explicit or irrelevent products don't appear on your Steam Store.

  • By tapping your user icon in the top right corner, tapping Account Details, and tapping Store Preferences , you will find a list of options that you can check or uncheck to make sure certain content does or does not appear on your Steam Store page.
