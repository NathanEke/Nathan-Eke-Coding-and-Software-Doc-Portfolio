Search Store

Search the Steam Store to find the perfect game that fits your interests.

  1. From the Store page, tap the search bar at the top of the screen.
  2. Type the name of the game or genre of your choice and tap Search . A list of games will appear.
  3. Scroll down the list until you find a game you like. The list will autopopulate with new results once you reach the bottom.
