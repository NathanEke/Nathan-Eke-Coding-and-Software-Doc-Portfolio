Refund a Game

If you regret a purchase, you may request a refund.

If you refund a game within 14 days of purchase and have less than 2 hours of total playtime, your refund will be automatically approved. All other refunds may be declined depending on reasoning. Refund requests are typically processed within 24 hours.
  1. In the top right corner, click your username.
  2. Click Account detailsin the dropdown menu.
  3. Click View purchase history.
  4. Select the purchase you'd like to refund from the list of options.
  5. Click I would like a refund.
  6. Click I'd like to request a refund.
  7. Select which payment method you'd like your refund to go to.
    1. Optionally, type in the Notes text box the exact reason for your refund request.
  8. Click Submit request.
