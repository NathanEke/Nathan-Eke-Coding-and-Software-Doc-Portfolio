Sort and Filter Store Search

When you search the Steam Store, you can sort and filter results based on certain criteria.


  • Sort
    • By tapping the dropdown labeled Sort by, you can sort search results by:
      • Release date
      • Relevance
      • Name
      • Lowest Price
      • Highest Price
      • User Reviews
      • Steam Deck Compatibility Review Date
  • Filter
    • When you search the Steam Store, a Filters button will appear at the top right of the screen and open a list of filtering options. Check or uncheck the box next to your preferred option to filter or unfilter your games based on that criteria.
