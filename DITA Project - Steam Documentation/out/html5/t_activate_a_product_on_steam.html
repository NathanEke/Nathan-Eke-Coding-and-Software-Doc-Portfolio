Activate a Product on Steam

Steam games that are bought from a third party seperate from the Steam Store must be manually activated using a code.

Activateable Steam games bought at a physical storefront will often have a code inside the box. Online third party sellers usually email you the code soon after purchase.

  1. From the Library page, click Add a Game at the bottom left.
  2. Click Activate a Product on Steam.
  3. Input the product code you received from the third party seller.
  4. Click Confirm.
