Steam Store

The Steam Store page is the primary hub where users can find and buy the latest games.

The Steam Store homepage offers game suggestions based on general popularity and a user's Library. The categories at the top curate games based on genre or features. The search bar at the top right allows users to find a specific title among the thousands available on Steam.

  • Store Preferences
    Is there certain content you don't want to see on your Steam Store page? Set your preferences so that explicit or irrelevent products don't appear on your Steam Store.
  • Search Store
    Search the Steam Store to find the perfect game that fits your interests.
  • Sort and Filter Store Search
    When you search the Steam Store, you can sort and filter results based on certain criteria.

  • Wishlist a Game
    If you want to keep an eye on a game, but don't yet want to buy it, you can use the wishlist feature.
  • Add Funds to Steam Wallet
    Add funds to your Steam Wallet to use it as an alternate payment option.
  • Buy a Game
    Once you've found a game on the Steam Store that is to your liking, you can purshcase it and add it toy your Library.
  • Refund a Game
    If you regret a purchase, you may request a refund.
