Sort and Filter Library

You can make it easier to find certain types of games in your Library by sorting and filtering.

  • Sort
    • By clicking the clock icon at the top of the left panel, you can sort your games based on recent activity.
  • Filter
    • By clicking the three bars icon next to the search bar, you can filter your games list based on a myriad of options. Check or uncheck the box next to your preferred option to filter or unfilter your games based on that criteria.
