Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Tape to Tape has actively engaged with the community since the earliest days of development. We strongly believe in the Early Access model and the benefits of continual design feedback from our community. We aim to keep communication open to continue improving the game as we move toward a full release.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We plan to keep the game in Early Access into late 2025 to gather as much feedback as possible and incorporate it into the game. The exact duration is not set in stone and will depend on how much we feel the need to adjust the gameplay or expand the game's scope before finalizing it.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“For the full version we are working toward a revamped Roguelite campaign, a draft mode, more teams, additional powerups and relics, fights, an overhauled UI, localization options, and a range of quality-of-life improvements. For more details, please follow our roadmap on Discord or here on Steam.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The Early Access version is fully playable and includes all content and features listed on the store page, such as: a 3-act roguelite campaign, tournament mode, 'play now' options with dozens of teams, and create-a-player/team customization features.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“There’s a possibility that the full version may be priced higher than the Early Access version, as the game will feature more content and expanded features upon full release.We believe Early Access offers players a chance to join the adventure early and help development at a lower price.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Player feedback is an integral part of our development process, and we greatly value every comment and suggestion we receive. Thanks to the support of our community, we've already made significant improvements throughout the Early Access journey. To provide your input and help us continue improving, we invite you to join our Discord server, where we are most active. Additionally, we will keep you updated on our progress and how your input is shaping the game through regular updates on both Steam and Discord.”

About This Game

Explore a gorgeous roguelite hockey world where every choice matters on your journey to restore hockey to its former glory. Hire unique players, manage your benchwarmers, discover synergies between them, choose new abilities, bribe the referees, visit the Blademaster for upgrades and a lot more.

CRAFT YOUR TEAM

Take charge of a ragtag group of hockey players in a roguelite journey across a mysterious land. As the newly appointed captain, every choice matters on your path to discover the secret of hockey's last bastion. Hire superstars, utilize the synergy found between players, and harness ruthless abilities, all in favor of crafting an unbeatable team.

LOSING IS ONLY THE BEGINNING

Easier said than done, as the ultimate dream team doesn’t appear out of thin air. Use losses as an opportunity to visit the mighty Blademaster in between runs and permanently upgrade your hero for the battles ahead. When the Center is still flubbing every single face-off, there’s no shame in lining the ref’s pockets with some sweet “maple syrup.”

DEEP AND REWARDING GAMEPLAY

Combining modern precision with fast-paced 90s-inspired action, Tape to Tape offers a unique experience in the form of a simcade hockey experience. Bone-crushing body checks and stellar saves will keep players shaking in their skates, but mastering the dynamic puck physics system will secure the victory. Players and goalies will react to the puck bouncing off sticks and ricocheting off faces creating organic and emergent battles on the ice. Thankfully, there is no penalty when a tooth is lost.

KEY FEATURES:

  • A deep Roguelite campaign featuring dozens of powerups, events, boss battles and unique players

  • Create + customize your teams and players with power-ups, artifacts, and personalized color options.

  • Over 40 different teams, each with unique synergies and colorful players

  • Local, couch and Steam Remote Play multiplayer for up to 4 players 

  • Innovative goaltenders that react organically to any shot, deflected puck or rebound -goals never feel unfair

  • Challenge your friends in Tournament, Play now or Shootout modes or play the Campaign cooperatively

  • A unique gorgeous blend of hand-drawn 2D sprites and animations in a 3D environment

4-PLAYER MULTIPLAYER!

The Tape to Tape can be played online with a total of up to 4 players using Steam remote play or locally. The fast-paced gameplay and tight controls makes for memorable matchups. We also invite you to join our Discord server to play with the dev team or to find other player amongst our wonderful community. The Roguelite campaign is also playable cooperatively with some friends for a truly unique experience.
