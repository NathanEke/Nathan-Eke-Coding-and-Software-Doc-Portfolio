Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“During the release of our first game, we learned the value of community feedback and understood that for this game to be the best it can be, it is crucial to include the players much earlier in the process. It is the perfect way for us to interact with fans and allows us to efficiently work on our game and shift it in the right direction.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Our current plan is to conclude the Early Access roadmap in 8 months. Of course, we need to be flexible and adapt development to community feedback, so this scope could be subject to change.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“Our goal for the Early Access release is to provide a stable, fun game loop that players can enjoy both by themselves or with friends. Our goal for the 1.0 version is to increase the replayability and variety by expanding on the game’s content with new characters, new spells, new worlds and new enemies.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The Early Access release has all the mechanics considered core to the game, presented in a finished structure that can be played alone or with friends. There are 3 worlds, each in 3 difficulties, 7 different enemies, 1 Boss, 8 playable Wizards, 10 Spells with 4 Elements and multiple Augmentations, a bunch of items and upgrades and stats, and, of course, awesome cosmetics for your Wizards. Players will unlock this gated content in a natural flow by completing missions.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to slightly raise the price of our game at full release.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“For us, the best way to funnel all the great feedback from players is on our Discord and right here on Steam. We keep a close eye on any discussions on the forums and personally moderate our Discord server, so any feedback regarding bugs, balancing or new content will be on our radar right away.”

About This Game

The Spell Brigade is an online co-op survivors-like for 1-4 players. Team up with your wizard friends to slay hordes of dark creatures. Complete team-based objectives, unlock new upgrades and create overpowered spell synergies. Friend nor foe is safe in this friendly fire bullet hell!



1-4 PLAYER CO-OP
The Spell Brigade combines the survivors-like gameplay you love with good old multiplayer madness. Fight thousands of weird monsters together. Complete random team-based objectives and upgrade spells together. Survive together!



FRIENDLY FIRE BULLET HEAVEN/HELL
Taking on entire armies is risky business, even with your friends at your side! Fighting as a team has many perks, but you will inevitably step on each other’s toes. But fear not! You can use your deaths to your advantage, and use Revive tokens to (literally) blast yourself and your teammates back into action!



MASTER YOUR SPELLS
Improve, augment and infuse your spells with different kinds of elements and upgrades. Combine this with your Enchantments, Quests, Relics, and the amount of wizards in your squad, and the potential for pure, unhinged chaos is endless!



JOIN OUR DISCORD
If you have any bugs to report or feedback to share, want to check in on the dev team or just talk about wizards, you can do all that and more on our awesome Discord! We also post dev content DAILY, so what are you waiting for?!
