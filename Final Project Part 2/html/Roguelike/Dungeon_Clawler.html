Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Our studio has a big focus on community-based development and this is already the fourth time we launch a game on Steam Early Access. All of our previous games greatly benefited from the experience. That’s why we have returned with this game to once again develop and improve it together with our players.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Dungeon Clawler is planned to stay in Early Access for one year or longer.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We plan to add new items, perks, characters, enemies and game mechanics for the full version of the game. We also plan to add Steam features such as trading cards and achievements. And finally, we're sure players will have lots of interesting ideas during Early Access which we will consider implementing as well.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current version of the game offers a complete deckbuilder roguelite experience with around 67 different items, 40 passive perks, 12 playable characters and 4 difficulty modes, allowing for many repeating runs using different strategies and builds.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price may increase moderately when we release out of Early Access.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We are planning to communicate with players about new features and ideas for the game and gather their feedback and bug reports in the forums and on Discord. We’re looking forward to improve the game together with you!”

About This Game

Dungeon Clawler mixes deck building with a dash of rogue-like mechanics and most importantly: a claw machine.

Find new items and add them to your claw machine to create game-changing builds. Earn gold coins to upgrade or modify your items and unlock new unknown synergies. You will need to use every trick up your sleeve to reach the evil dungeon lord’s lair at the heart of the dungeon and reclaim your lost paw.

  • The ultimate claw machine roguelike experience.

  • Unique characters each with their different strengths and weaknesses.

  • Multiple difficulty levels and unlockable lucky paws to customize your run.

  • Break the game – go crazy with different claw and item synergies.

  • Venture into mystery locations and discover treasures as well as strange and dangerous obstacles.

  • Spend your hard earned coins in the dungeon's pachinko machines.

  • Meet different characters who might help you on your journey.
