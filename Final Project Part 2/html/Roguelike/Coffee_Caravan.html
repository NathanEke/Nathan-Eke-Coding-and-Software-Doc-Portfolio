About This Game

In Coffee Caravan you'll immerse yourself in the world of a cafe manager, tasked with brewing up a variety of coffee, tea and even ice cream delights and building your own coffee business on wheels. The game features a charming 3D environment with a pastel-colored aesthetic, and your daily journey takes you on a procedurally generated road trip full of surprises. Throughout your adventure, you'll discover and unlock new recipes, purchase cutting-edge appliances, and focus on providing an efficient and enjoyable experience for your customers.

Manage

  • Strategically choose the recipes you want to serve each day, catering to customer preferences and experimenting with new flavors.

  • Invest in a variety of equipment to improve efficiency, unlock new brewing methods, and enhance the quality of your products.

  • Balance the speed and quality of service to keep customers happy and earning more money.

  • Manage your finances wisely, allocating earnings to buy new recipes and upgrade equipment.

Serve

  • Effeciently juggle multiple customer orders to ensure you serve the right products and meet their unique preferences.

  • Keep an eye on each customer’s patience meter, as long waits can lead to dissatisfaction and end of the run.

  • Choose premium recipes to earn more money per cup, but serve fewer customers to keep the game manageable.

Coffee World

  • Explore an extensive tree of coffee recipes, each with its own unique combination of ingredients and brewing techniques.

  • Upgrade and maintain various coffee-making appliances, from espresso machines to pour-over setups, to unlock new brewing methods and enhance flavor profiles.

  • Master the art of efficient brewing, balancing speed and quality to serve a growing line of customers and maximize your profits.

Tea World

  • Explore the rich world of tea recipes, where every type of tea requires a unique blend of leaves, ingredients, and brewing methods.

  • Master the art of preparing traditional matcha, discover the subtleties of steeping black tea, or learn to create delicious bubble tea in a variety of flavors.

  • Upgrade your tea-making equipment, from matcha preparation tools to modern bubble tea machines, and enhance your tea craft one step at a time.

Ice Cream World

  • Discover the diverse world of ice cream recipes. Master the craft of creating classic scoopable ice cream with a smooth, creamy texture, blending refreshing milkshakes, or making fruity popsicles and sorbets that cool you off on the hottest days.

  • Bake crispy cookies for ice cream sandwiches and experiment with unique flavors that will delight every sweet tooth.

  • Perfect your mixing and freezing techniques to achieve the ideal consistency and taste that will satisfy all foodies. Continuously upgrade your equipment, from ice cream machines to baking tools, and offer new, innovative experiences like the Italian affogato, combining ice cream with espresso.

Explore

  • Each playthrough offers a unique road trip experience, ensuring that no two journeys are the same.

  • Choose customers at different levels of difficulty, from easy levels for learning the basics to challenging hard levels that cater to more sophisticated tastes.

  • Find scattered appliance and recipe shops along the way to purchase and upgrade your equipment.

  • Encounter special events and challenges along the way, from rewards to unexpected circumstances, to add excitement to your road trip adventure.

  • The final boss customers are the epitome of taste perfectionism, setting the highest standard for your coffee-making skills in the grand finale of the journey.
