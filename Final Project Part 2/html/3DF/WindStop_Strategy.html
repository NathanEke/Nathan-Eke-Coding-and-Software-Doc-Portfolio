About This Game

Currently, the development of "Windstop Strategy" is in full sprint mode. Below is our development and update plan before the official release. We invite all leaders to review it.

Also, an important reminder three times: no EA, no EA, no EA!

——————————

A sudden and devastating massacre of your sect completely overturns your previously tranquil life, the young master of Hu Yan Men - SITU LAIYE.
As the new head of Hu Yan Men, could you stem the surging tide, stop short before falling off a cliff?

In the ever-changing landscape of Windstop Strategy, where conflicts escalate and rumors abound. A newcomer to Jianghu, can you find a way to break free from the encirclement of various factions?

Amidst the legend of a ferocious beast known as "Night Crow," can you cut though the full of confusion, expose the true mastermind to revenge.

Windstop Strategy employs classic region suppression as the core gameplay strategy,

while also integrating a large number of management and simulation elements revolving around sects.

Players need to judiciously consider each move during their own turns, making strategic use of available resources.

Develop your strongholds and recruit talents! Analyze the situation and deploy the strategies! Take attack or fall back upon, step by step!

Starting from Hu Yan Hall, conquer the various sects of Windstop Strategy!

Thus the highest form of generalship is to baulk the enemy's plans; the next best is to prevent the junction of the enemy's forces

Jostling and joyous, the whole world comes for profit; after profit the whole world goes!

In Jianghu, where heroes vie, driven by self-interest, there is no such thing as 'absolute'.

Therefore, when facing a formidable enemy, should one choose war or peace? In times of crisis, who truly deserves to be trusted as an ally?

The situation in Windstop Strategy changes rapidly; only those who can perceive everything clearly can survive.

The combat phase of Windstop Strategy will be presented in a "pre-deployment-focused auto-battler" mode.

Players can seize opportunities by manually using characters' skills,

or by issuing detailed tactical commands through the command system.

The combat system will provide rich strategic depth and replayability.

All characters have their own independent growth systems, strengths and weaknesses based on their combat styles.

Different cultivating directions will largely determine their roles in combat.

Please remember, only a logical character build and team composition can ensure victory for the team.

Threat and opportunity, intrigue and betrayal, hatred and bonds, love and friendship.

Since the moment you decided to step into Jianghu, it seems as if everything has been destined.

The intertwining of light and shadow, the intricately complex storyline, the ups and downs.

Lots of suspense will guide you to explore the decades-old pains and sorrows between the major sects of Windstop Strategy.

Tired of constant scheming and battles between sects? Perhaps it's time to embark on a true adventure!

In addition to the strategic gameplay unfolding around the world map, Windstop Strategy also provides players with a variety of special dungeons to explore.

From ancient ruins filled with traps, to serene valleys rarely visited by humans, and dangerous territories occupied by bandits.

As you delve into these dungeons, you'll experience unique storylines and battles, and gain abundant rewards.
