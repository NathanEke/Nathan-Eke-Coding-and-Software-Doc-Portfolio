About This Game

Wrap yourself in 5 tons of metal and go smashing through a dark scene of

UNDERGROUND MECH FIGHTS!

SMASH, BASH, CRUSH

You are a giant metal ape - act like it.

Rampage through hordes of enemy bots and mechs in unhinged, physics-based, metal-on-metal violence!

GEAR UP!

This is a rogue-like with 130+ (!) items, weapons and power tools.

Build the most overpowered, imbalanced killing machine, and break the game!

IT'S A DANGEROUS JUNGLE

This city's run by gangsters and psychos.

Hustle and deal with 40+ NPCs, hire hackers and saboteurs, get into street fights, steal, and sell people out.

You do what you need to do.

YOU AIN'T ALONE

Luckily, your little bruv King is in your corner - watching your back and calling things out. After all, you're doing this all for him. For a chance to get him into the city and save his life.

So wassup?! You still reading?

Step up and fight like a wild animal, smashing car sized pit bots that wanna kill you dead to the deafening roars of the blood thirsty crowds.

Step up to New Brakka - The Mech Fighting Capital of the World.

Step up to UNDERDOGS.

Mature Content Description

The developers describe the content like this:

the game has to parts to it - the combat part which depicts metal on metal action, with no flesh and bone human/animal involved, and the tale part which is illustrated by 2D art, and that shows gangster-vibe violence from time to time. its important to note that it doesn't depict any kind of sexual violence, drug and alcohol abuse or self harm of any kind.

System Requirements
