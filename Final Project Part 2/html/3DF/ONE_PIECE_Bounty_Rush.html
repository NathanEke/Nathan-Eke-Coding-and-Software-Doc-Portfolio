About This Game

Take the loot, pirates!

- Victory rests on the berry in your hand!
Bounty Rush is the first ONE PIECE 4 vs 4 multiplayer team action game.
In this game, working with your team is the key to victory!
Compete in real-time, with complete synchronization, against an enemy team and aim to steal their berries!

- The world of ONE PIECE has been transformed into a 3D battlefield!
The stages are filled with items that give you and your team an advantage, as well as obstacles that get in your way.
Use whatever you can get your hands on and lead your team to victory!

- Emerge victorious using distinctive ONE PIECE characters in Team Battle!
In this mode, you can control popular ONE PIECE characters and battle against rival teams.
Characters have different strengths too: those who excel at advancing the frontlines and those who are better at taking the defensive in the rear.
Pick your playstyle and charge into battle!

System Requirements

    Minimum:
    • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
    • OS: Windows 10 / Windows 11
    • Processor: AMD Ryzen 3 1200 / Intel Core i5-2300
    • Memory: 4 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 460 / Radeon HD 7770
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 7 GB available space
    • Additional Notes: Estimated performance: 1080p/60fps with graphics settings at "Low". Framerate might drop in graphics-intensive scenes. - 64-bit processor and operating system are required.
    Recommended:
    • Requires a 64-bit processor and operating system
    • OS: Windows 10 / Windows 11
    • Processor: AMD Ryzen 3 1200 / Intel Core i5-2300
    • Memory: 8 GB RAM
    • Graphics: Nvidia GeForce GTX 660 Ti / Radeon R9 270 / Intel Arc A310
    • DirectX: Version 11
    • Storage: 7 GB available space
    • Additional Notes: Estimated performance: 1080p/60fps with graphics settings at "High". Framerate might drop in graphics-intensive scenes. - 64-bit processor and operating system are required. - Windows 10 (Version 1809 or later) and a 4GB VRAM GPU (graphics board or video card) are required for DirectX 12 API.
