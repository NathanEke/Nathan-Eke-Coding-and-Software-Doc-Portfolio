Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“In 2023, I released 2 demos of BKSB that received overwhelming support from boxing fans; so much that some were taken advantage of by scammers selling pirated copies. As I continue to reach new heights in my journey as a game developer, I've realized that I'll be polishing this game for quite some time and the fans are really eager for more. Early Access is the answer to everyone's prayers!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“BSKB will be in Early Access until the list of tasks on the roadmap have been completed. The roadmap can be viewed in my discord in the "bksb-roadmap" channel. I estimate these goals to be completed by June 2025.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“In the full version of this game, I aim to provide an immersive experience from the perspective of an active boxer. Adding my own twist to Career Mode, you'll be able to train your fighter via mini games that simulate boxing drills. There will be tons of customization options for your fighters appearance, as well. This will be the type of game that you can play for hours whether you're gaming Online or having a few friends over. I have a very unorthodox vision for the future of the game but I must keep quite. No need to ruin your excitement.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The current state of BKSB has a solid foundation of realistic gameplay and fun game modes that will keep the fans busy while it continues to develop. A list of these features can be seen in the about me section of the store page. Local multiplayer is sure to be a hit!”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“The price is subject to change as progression is made but nothing is set in stone as of now.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“The BKSB community has been paramount in the development journey of this game. I will continue to accept suggestions from them via Discord and my personal YouTube channel where I've produced a devlog series that gives players a deep dive into the production process.”

About This Game

Bloody Knuckles Street Boxing is a physics based boxing game made by a long time boxing enthusiast who also enjoys gritty fighting games. It's time to put your boxing IQ to the test.... IN THE STREETS!

Here are some things that you will experience:

GAMEPLAY
- Total control movement system with user controlled lateral movement and pivots.
- Over 30 punch animations with user controlled target tracking.
- Adjust defensively by switching your guard in real time.
- Highly effective counter punching from slick head movement and parries.
- Organic clinch system with realistic physics.
- AI sparring with AI action presets to help you master your strategy.
- Training games including shadow boxing and FOCUS MITTS!
- SATISFYING RAGDOLL KNOCKOUTS!
