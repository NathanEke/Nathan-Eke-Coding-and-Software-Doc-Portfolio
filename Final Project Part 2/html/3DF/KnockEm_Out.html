Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Knock’em out is a funny and bloody physics brawler game that has a particular design that allows it to scale very easy adding new content. My main goal is to stay active on the title when Early Access it’s launched, adding a lot of content like:
- New Maps
- New Weapons and Powerups
- New stage traps
- New costumes for the characters
- More gamemodes
Moreover, I would like to hear feedback from the users about the gameplay, and trying to make the game more enjoyable for them. For now the game is very enjoyable, but far from realizing its potential.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“My plan is being in Early Access around one year or a year and a half”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version is planned to have a much larger number of features. The features I plan to add are:
- At least 10 playable levels with their own traps
- A larger set of costumes for the players, and a Customizable menu to mix them
- More than 10 weapons to allow the players to kill the other players in a lot of crazy ways.
- At least 10 game modes
- Polished gameplay with player feedback”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“The early access currently has:
- Online Multiplayer & Local Multiplayer with other players and AI
- 5 Maps
- 10 Different bloody traps
- 6 Weapons
- 8 sets of costumes
- 3 Game Modes”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“I plan to launch the game at a lower price, and to rise it when big changes are added”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“As the game needs player feedback to grow up I will try handle several ways to make it possible:
- I plan to be reading feedback from the Steam community forums and trying to answer possible customer doubts.
- I plan to handle a discord server where players can share anything about the game like screenshots, funny moments, videos or bugs. Also we plan to hear any feedback or suggestion from the players and make the game grow together.
- I plan to be sharing the development through social media and Steam News.
- I plan to be hosting some events during Early Access like tournaments so that players feel more involved in the game.”

About This Game

Be a BOXI and tear your opponents' bodies apart while preventing them from shredding your muscular character in this physics-based multiplayer brawl.

Use the stage traps to become the champion of the ring.

Keep a cool head to knock out your opponents in the most violent and fun way!

Features:

  • Split-screen multiplayer and online mode.

  • Remote Play Together support.

  • Play with controller or keyboard.

  • Customize your Boxi.

  • Several game modes such as 2 vs 2 or up to 8 players all against all.

  • Combat system based on physics.

  • Variety of scenarios to fight.

  • Multitude of trapsand weapons in each scenario.

  • Achievement system.

Among the game modes that will be available in Knock'Em Out we highlight:

  • Deathmatch: Knock out all the players until you are the last one standing

  • Bomb Tag: Escape from the player with the bomb, the last one standing will win

[/list].

  • Title Champion: Take the title and defend it in order to become the champion
