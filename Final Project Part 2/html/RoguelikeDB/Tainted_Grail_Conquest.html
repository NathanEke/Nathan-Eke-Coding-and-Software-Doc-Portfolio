TOP BUNDLE



Set foot onto a world full of darkness and treachery, where danger lurks around every corner after King Arthur's demise. Roam the island of Avalon, battle your way through numerous enemies and live a story out of twisted Arthurian legends, unlike any other.

This bundle contains two games from the Tainted Grail franchise, which first took the world by storm as a board game. Tainted Grail: The Fall of Avalon (early access) and Tainted Grail: Conquest. Both let you immerse yourself into the dark island of Avalon's setting in different ways and both guarantee a journey you won't soon forget.

This bundle includes:
Tainted Grail: The Fall of AvalonStep into a dark reimagination of Arthurian legends in this first person, open world RPG. Explore a world stuck in everlasting autumn, witness falling legends and make meaningful choices woven into a complex, branching storyline.

Tainted Grail: Conquest

A unique, infinitely replayable, story-driven hybrid between a deck-building Roguelike and an RPG game. Explore the ever-changing maps, fight with deadly enemies, and learn what happened to the cursed island of Avalon.
