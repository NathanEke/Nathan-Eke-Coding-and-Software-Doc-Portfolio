About This Game

Assemble your team of heroes, unlock synergy buffs, and craft rare artifacts. Choose powerful combat perks to enhance your builds, then challenge other players and conquer new lands!

Team Building

In Valefor, you fight with a team equipped with powerful items. The heroes you choose matter, as each has unique abilities and belongs to a specific class and race. The smarter you equip and position your heroes, the better your chances of victory.

Summon and Craft

Collect fragments from loot or buy them in the shop, and use them to summon units and craft equipment. Combine them to create even stronger heroes and artifacts.

Ranked PVP

Fight against real party builds, made by real players, in our PVP Arena. Ascend the ranks in true roguelike fashion, with a new team built from scratch every run. Analyze the strategy used by your opponents, press advantages and try to counter them. Take your time experimenting with the vast array of heroes, perks and items at your disposal because - with Asynchronous PVP - there is no turn timer.

Solo Campaign

Valefor is a Roguelite Strategy Auto-Battle RPG that is lightweight enough for portable play, and deep enough for tactical gameplay.  Step into a rich dark fantasy world and follow the fragmented story in a single player campaign building your Kingdom along the way.

Expand your Kingdom

Every class and faction in the game has its own unique Building that can be constructed and upgraded to unlock powerful synergy buffs, give heroes class specific starting equipment and can even increase max party sizes.  Defeat the dungeons that have taken hold of the land to unlock more space to grow your Kingdom.

Epic Soundtrack and Lore

On top of Valefor’s sleek visuals- we’ve also included a soundtrack and story to match.  Whether it’s an epic score, a mournful ambience, or an action-packed battle scene- our music and lore seek to immerse you in our world and enhance your gaming experience.

Active Development

Here at Valefor, we are constantly working to make this game the best we can- and we can’t do that without you. Have ideas, critiques, bug reports or just want to chat?  Check out our Discord - we are incredibly active there. You can also visit our site, join our newsletter for development updates- or check out our many social accounts.    We appreciate your support!
