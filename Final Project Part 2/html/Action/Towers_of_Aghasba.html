Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: This Early Access game is not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“Towers of Aghasba is an ambitious open-world sandbox developed by a small team with core mechanics intended to let players shape their island however they like. Involving our community in the development process will better allow us to balance, polish, and iterate on the core components of the game, as well as introduce new content and features based directly on player feedback.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“We expect Towers of Aghasba to be in Early Access for around 12-18 months to work in player feedback and incorporate all the content originally envisioned for the game.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We expect the full version of Towers of Aghasba to have a more complete and polished set of features and content. The game world will be expanded with more biomes, ecosystems, and village types compared to the Early Access version. The quest-line will also cover more narrative ground and reach a more dramatic conclusion in time, and the maximum number of players allowed in multiplayer sessions will increase.

During Early Access, we plan to release periodic major updates to introduce new features, areas, characters, cutscenes, and content. We will also be releasing consistent patches focusing on bug fixes and existing content polish.

Our public roadmap will provide more context on what is included in each update.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Towers of Aghasba launches in Early Access with six (6) regions to explore, three (3) types of ecosystems to grow, and three (3) types of villages/settlements to develop. There are over 50 creatures to discover and over 150 decorations to craft. The current quest-line ends with a satisfying conclusion that hints at narrative developments to come. Multiplayer play is currently capped at 4 players per session, who are able to convene on one player’s island to explore and build together.

While our Early Access launch is intended to provide a comprehensive and fun experience, our game is far from complete. There are several new features, quests, and more in development that we will be releasing alongside regular bug fixes and improvements based on constructive feedback from our player community.

Throughout the Early Access period, you may see improvements to the game’s controls, UI, and gameplay systems as you install updates.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We plan to increase the game’s retail price at the end of the Early Access period.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We’ll be actively monitoring all forms of feedback from our player community as we continue building on all aspects of Towers of Aghasba.

Feedback provided on our official Discord server, Steam discussion forums, and other social channels will all be reviewed and considered. We encourage our community to provide honest feedback about their experiences so that we can ensure we are creating the best game possible.”

About This Game

Decades after the Shimu people were forced to flee their homeland due to the invasion of a mysterious force known as the Withered, the Shimu have returned to reclaim their rightful home: the fantastical island of AGHASBA. As junior architect, it’s your job to rebuild the villages of your once-mighty civilization as well as nurse Aghasba’s once-thriving ecosystems back to life.

EXPLORE THE ISLAND OF AGHASBA
Traverse varied terrain, scale colossal mountains and gaze out upon jaw-dropping vistas, all brought to life in a vibrant, high-fidelity art style. Befriend strange and memorable creatures, learn the storied history of the Shimu people and uncover the dark origins of the Withered.

REBUILD THE SHIMU CIVILIZATION

Build sprawling settlements and watch them come alive as they’re inhabited by members of the Shimu tribe. Lose yourself in a rich creative sandbox as you decorate and construct your villages in a way that’s uniquely you.

RESTORE AGHASBA’S ECOSYSTEMS

Restore life to a devastated land by nurturing the growth of beautifully bizarre ecosystems. Grow plant life, attract & feed animals, and work with the gods of Aghasba to revitalize the island.

BRAVE THE WITHERED LANDS

Much of Aghasba is still infested with the Withered, a dark force that absorbs the life from all it touches. Craft weapons, armor and elixirs before venturing into the Withered Lands and battle bloodthirsty Withered monsters, gradually ridding the island of the Withered plague.

INVITE FRIENDS TO YOUR ISLAND

Once you build your villages and grow your ecosystems how you want, it’s time to show them off! Invite up to 3 friends to explore your island, trade items, hunt creatures and battle the Withered as a team.

EXPLORE

TOGETHER
