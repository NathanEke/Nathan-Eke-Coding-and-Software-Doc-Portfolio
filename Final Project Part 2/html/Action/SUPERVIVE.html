About This Game

SUPERVIVE is a MOBA BR meets HERO SHOOTER.

Come experience this world first combination of speed, precision, combat, strategy, and teamplay for free.

WHAT’S IN THE GAME (THIS LIST WILL GET BIGGER EVERY WEEK):

* Enter a wild, open-ended combat sandbox where you’ll jump, glide, shoot, punch, bounce, nuke, and spike your enemies to be the last team standing

* Multiple game modes: squads BR (4 players per team), duos BR (2 players per team), arena (4v4), and special event modes

* 16+ storm-chasing hunters to play, including a mecha-suited pirate hunter, a fox with a flamethrower, and an anarchist who commands the power of lightning

* SO MANY powers and equipment to chase every match for that perfect build - nuke your enemies, blast them with lasers, rewind time, or become a tree (you heard us)

* A billion ways to come back and win: you’re never out until you’re out

* From a studio of veteran devs that have built some of the biggest PvP games in the world and will be evolving SUPERVIVE for years to come with you
