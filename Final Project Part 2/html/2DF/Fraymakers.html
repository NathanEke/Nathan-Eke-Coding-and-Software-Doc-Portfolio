Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We plan to release Fraymakers in Early Access on January 18, 2023. While the full content for the game will take a bit longer, we’ll be prioritizing a specific set of content and features to get the game in your hands as soon as possible. Our Early Access release will feature, at minimum, 4 Characters, 5 Stages, 20 Assists, Rollback Online and FrayTools, our custom content program. As the developers of a high-profile fangame, we're quite experienced with the type of ongoing development cycle that comes with releasing a game in early form.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“2 years.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“The full version will have more character, assist, and stage content, as well as fleshed out modes.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Upon initial release, Early Access will feature the following content at minimum:

- 4 Playable Characters
- 5 Stages
- 20 Assists
- Rollback Online
- User-Generated Content Online
- Integration with our external content editor, FrayTools”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Purchasing during Early Access will guarantee you a discounted price from the final product. While we will not gradually raise the price during development, when the full version of the game is released the price of the game will be increased to reflect the value of all of the content added from Early Access up to that point.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Our goal with Early Access is to involve our players in the development process to help us make Fraymakers the best game it can be. We will work with our community of players to tune all of our content over the course of Early Access. This includes leveraging our Steam Community in addition to our public Discord server, both of which allow direct interaction between you and our development team.

We plan to use our players’ feedback to help identify and address bugs, tune gameplay balance, enhance editor functionality and adjust our content focus areas. We’re open to suggestions and willing to make significant changes to Fraymakers based on what our players are looking for. By participating in our communities, you have the opportunity to give valuable feedback with a real, tangible impact on the final product.

Discord: https://discord.gg/mcleodgaming”

About This Game

What is Fraymakers?

Fraymakers is the ultimate customizable platform fighting game, featuring a cast of some of indie gaming's biggest icons. Duke it out with up to 4 players*, and call on assists to help out in the heat of battle. Create or play custom characters, stages, modes, and more for infinite fun!

Key Traits

  • Easy to pick up, hard to master - Fraymakers is designed to be friendly to new players and challenging and expressive for veteran players. You’re going to have a ton of fun whether you want a quick round with your friends or you’re looking to climb the leaderboards.
  • Rollback Netcode - If you’ve been part of the fighting game community over the past few years, you know that rollback is the gold standard for fighting game netcode. We’ve prioritized and designed around rollback from the outset of development, meaning that Fraymakers will provide the smoothest possible online experience.
  • Full Movesets - With over 80 animations per character, we've skipped nothing! Each character comes complete with every motion and attack you've come to expect from a platform fighter as well as some brand new techniques.
  • Assists - Choose from a massive selection of indie characters to unleash an attack, fire a projectile, buff your character, or inflict a special status effect on your foes.
  • ...And Infinite Expandability! - We designed both the game and our custom content editor to be accessible and powerful. We want the process of creating your own content to be simple and fun. Whether you're a player or a creator, this means that the possibilities are endless.

* Local multiplayer requires the use of a gamepad for each additional player (and keyboard may be used for one player slot)
