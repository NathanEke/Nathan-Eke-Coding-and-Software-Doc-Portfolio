About This Game

Skill Legends Royale is a free multiplayer PVP strategy game.

Blending rogue-like and auto chess elements, it delivers a fresh gaming experience.

Players must equip their auto-battling heroes with the right treasures and skills to outlast all other contenders and secure victory.

12 Unique Archetypes

*The game features 12 distinct archetypes, with more to come in future updates.

Random Selection of 8 Archetypes

*Each match features 8 randomly selected archetypes, ensuring a unique experience every time.

Freely Combine Archetypes

*Different archetypes complement each other—experiment to craft the most powerful combination.

Rich Content

*Over 100 skills for endless build possibilities.

*60+ treasures to mix and match.

*30+ unique heroes, each with distinct traits.

Auto Battler-like

*Eight players face off online, with everyone fending for themselves.

Easy to Pick Up

*Everything can be randomized, and heroes auto-battle, letting you play with ease.

Thank you for playing our game—we hope it brings you joy.

We first developed this gameplay in the Warcraft 3 Editor, where it was enjoyed by over 300,000 players on the KK platform.

With over 10,000 reviews and a 9.5 rating, it’s the authentic original “Electronic Fighting Insects” experience.

We are committed to keeping the game fresh with continuous updates and look forward to your support.
