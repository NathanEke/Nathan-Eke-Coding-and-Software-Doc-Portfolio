Customize a Wallpaper

Once you've subscribed to a wallpaper, you can use a limited array of customization options to tailor it to your liking.

When you select a wallpaper, a number of customization options will appear under "Properties" in the preview panel. Options vary. Here are the most common ones:

  • Audio Recording: Determines whether the wallpaper will display soundwaves based on your computer audio.
  • Scheme Color: Controls the accent color of Windows.
  • Playback Rate: Controls how fast a wallpaper's animation plays.
  • Alignment: Controls how the wallpaper aligns to your desktop screen.
  • Position: Controls the horizontal positoning of the wallpaper relative to your desktop.
  • Flip: Flips the perspective of the wallpaper.
  • Show Color Options: Displays brightness, contrast, saturation, and hue shift sliders for that wallpaper.

Move on to Save a Preset if you wish to save your customized wallpaper for later use.
