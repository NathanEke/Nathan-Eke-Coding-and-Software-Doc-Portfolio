Unsubscribe to a Wallpaper

If at any point you no longer wish to use a wallpaper and want it removed from your “Installed” directory, you can unsubscribe.

  1. In your “Installed” directory, click on the wallpaper you wish to unsubscribe to.
  2. Click the red “unsubscribe” button on the right.
  3. Read the text that appears and, if you still want to unsubscribe, click “OK.”
