Search for a Wallpaper

The Workshop is where users upload and share their wallpapers with others. Search the Workshop to find the right wallpaper for you. Or search your “Installed” directory to find wallpapers you’re already subscribed to.

  1. Ensure that the Steam application is open. You may skip this step if you are searching your “Installed” directory.
  2. Choose a directory
    • If searching for a new wallpaper, click “Workshop” in the top left corner.
    • If searching for wallpapers you already subscribed to, click “Installed” in the top left corner.
  3. Click the search bar in the top left corner
  4. Enter the name of a design you want featured in the wallpaper (ex. Cars, space, soccer, etc.). Results will populate the middle of the application.
    • If you want to further narrow down results, go to Refine Search.
  5. Scan the results for a wallpaper you like.
    • Click on a wallpaper to get a preview of it on the right side of the application.
  6. Once you’ve found a wallpaper you like, you may move on to Subscribe to a Wallpaper.
