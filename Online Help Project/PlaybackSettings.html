Playback Settings

Playback Settings determine when a wallpaper's animations are allowed to play.

By default, Wallpaper Engine's sounds and animations are constanty running regardless of what you are doing on your PC. You can change your Playback Settings so that animations mute, pause, or stop entirely depending on what other applications are doing on your PC. This can free up RAM and generally improve your computer's performance while using Wallpaper Engine. Here are the configurable settings available to you:

  • Other Application Focused: Determines wallpaper behavior when another application is focused on screen.
  • Other Application Maximized: Determines wallpaper behavior when another application is maximized on screen.
  • Other Application Fullscreen: Determines wallpaper behavior when another application is fullscreen.
  • Other Application Playing Audio: Determines wallpaper behavior when another application is playing audio.
  • Display Asleep: Determines wallpaper behavior when the computer's display is asleep.
  • Laptop on Battery: Determines wallpaper behavior when laptop is running on battery power.
  • Application Rules: Let's you change wallpaper behavior for when specific applications are running.
