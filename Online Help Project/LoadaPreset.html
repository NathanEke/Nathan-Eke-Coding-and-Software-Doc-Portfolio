Load a Preset

When you're ready to use a saved preset, you can use the "Load a Preset" option.

  1. Select the wallpaper you want to apply the preset to and scroll down to "Your Presets."
  2. Click "Load a Preset."
  3. Click the name of the preset you want to apply, then click "OK."
    • Alternatively, you can double-click the name of the preset.
