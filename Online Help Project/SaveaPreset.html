Save a Preset

Once you've customized a wallpaper to your liking, you can save that configuration for later use as a preset.

  1. In your “Installed” directory, click on the wallpaper that you'd like to save a preset for.
  2. Customize the properties to your liking. For more information, see Customize a Wallpaper.
  3. Scroll down the properties and find the "Your Presets" section.
  4. Click "Save" under "Your Presets."
  5. Enter a name for the preset.
  6. Click "OK." You can now load that preset at anytime for that wallpaper. For more information on loading a preset, check Load a Preset.
