Subscribe to a Wallpaper

Once you have found a wallpaper you like from the Workshop, it’s time to apply it to your desktop. To do this, you must subscribe to a wallpaper. Subscribing to a wallpaper downloads it to your computer and applies the wallpaper to your desktop. From then on, that wallpaper can be found under your “Installed” wallpapers in the top right.

  1. In the Workshop, click the wallpaper you want to subscribe to.
  2. Click the green “subscribe” button on the right. It will take time to download based on your internet speed and the size of the wallpaper.

Alternatively,

  1. In the Workshop, double-click on the wallpaper you want to subscribe to. You will automatically be subscribed to the wallpaper.
