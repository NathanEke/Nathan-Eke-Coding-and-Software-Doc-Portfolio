Create Hotkeys

To quickly access Wallpaper Engine functions, you can create hotkeys.

Hotkeys allow you to quickly alter Wallpaper Engine settings using a combination of keys rather than by going through the UI.

  1. Click "Settings" at the top of the application.
  2. Click "General."
  3. Next to "Hotkeys," click "Edit."
  4. Choose an option you'd like to make a hotkey for and click the box next to it.
    • NOTE: All hotkey combinations must include Ctrl, Shift, or Alt.
  5. Enter the combination of keys that you prefer then click "OK." You'll now be able to perform that action using the hotkey you created.
