Create a Playlist

Create a group of wallpapers that you'd like to cycle through regularly or on the fly.

  1. From the "Installed" directory, click on a wallpaper you want to add to the playlist.
  2. At the bottom of the application, click the white "+" with the blue background. A playlist menu will open.
  3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 for however many wallpapers you want added to the playlist.
    • By default, the playlist will cycle to a new wallpaper every hour. If you'd like to learn more about how to configure this timing and other settings, check out Configure Playlist Settings.
  4. Once you've selected all the wallpapers you desire, click the floppy disc icon in the playlist menu.
  5. Assign a name to the playlist.
    • If you want to cycle through wallpapers in the playlist on the fly, you can also assign a hotkey to the playlist. Hotkeys can be any combination of keys that includes the Ctrl key.
  6. Click "OK." You can now view and switch playlists by clicking the folder icon at the bottom of the application and double-clicking the name of the playlist.
