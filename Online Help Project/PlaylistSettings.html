Playlist Settings

Once you've created a playlist, you can use a limited array of customization options to tailor it to your liking.

When you select a playlist, click on the set of gears icon in the playlist bar to open Playlist Settings. Here are some common options you can customize:

  • Change Wallpaper: This dictates what triggers the playlist to switch to a new wallpaper.
  • Order: Dictates whether wallpapers are cycled through randomly or by how they are ordered in the playlist bar. You can move a wallpaper to a new position in the playlist bar by clicking and dragging it.
  • Show Wallpaper Transition: Play a brief animation when transitioning to a new wallpaper.
