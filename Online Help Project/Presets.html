Presets

Presets allow you freely customize your wallpapers and quickly apply those settings to others.

Given the bevy of options for customizing a wallpaper, you may want to have multiple configurations available that you can switch to on the fly. Presets allow you to do just that. You can save multiple presets and load them later at your discresion. You may find preset options at the bottom of the preview panel when you select a wallpaper.
