Welcome to Wallpaper Engine

Wallpaper Engine is the number #1 choice for sprucing up your desktop.

Wallpaper Engine is a paid application on Steam that lets you apply custom animated images (wallpapers) to your desktop background. You can also create, edit, and share these wallpapers through the Wallpaper Engine Workshop page. To begin your journey using Wallpaper Engine, continue on to our Table of Contents.
