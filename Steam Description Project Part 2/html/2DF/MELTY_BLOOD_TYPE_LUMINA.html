About This Game

MELTY BLOOD is reborn.

Based on Tsukihime: A Piece of Blue Glass Moon, this is new 2D fighting game, featuring new scenarios and characters.

Iconic Battle Systems

In addition to the systems inherited from the MELTY BLOOD series, it has new systems such as Moon Skill, that unleashes powerful techniques with simple inputs. We have pursued a battle system that allows you to enjoy intense battles with simple controls, that can be enjoyed not only by experienced players, but also by beginners who will touch MELTY BLOOD for the first time.



Online battles using Rollback Netcode

MELTY BLOOD: TYPE LUMINA utilizes Rollback Netcode for stable online battles against other players.

All-New Stories, Visuals, & Characters

Explore an all-new MELTY BLOOD world from scenario writer Kinoko Nasu.

Unique story events for each character give each playthrough its own feel, adding to the overall experience. The dialogue is fully voiced and changes depending on the combination of characters during battle, giving more insight into their relationship.

In addition, the visuals have been renewed for the HD environment. And each character's special move has beautiful animation produced by A-1 Pictures.

Rich Customization & Gallery Feature

Customize your title, profile icon, and character outfits for battle. They can even be used in online matches!

Collect visuals, opening cinematics, music tracks, voices, pixel art, and more in the gallery. You’ll even be able to look at guest illustrations created by artists connected to TYPE-MOON titles.

Easy-to-play game structure for beginners to advanced players

In addition to tutorials where you can learn the basics of the game, we also have missions for each character for those who want to further master the game!
