About This Game

Into the Dungeon is a roguelike deck builder with online coop. Play as one of 4 classes to battle creatures from a grim fantasy world tainted by evil. Build your deck with class unique cards, unlock artifacts that augment the way you play, and team-up with cross class synergies.

Into the Dungeon features 4 unique character classes, each with their own unique perks, cards and playstyles.

Classes

  • The Wizard deals high damage and manipulates the Exhaust card mechanic to his advantage. His unique class artifact the Mana Stone carries mana over through rounds allowing him to deal massive damage with high cost cards.

  • The Paladin can sustain heavy damage with his unique class artifact Plate Armor. He utilizes combos between offensive and defensive cards to deal heavy damage while maintaining his defensive stats.

  • The Viking deals massive damage through single turn combos while granting herself and her allies damage buffs and resistances. Her unique class artifact the Three Leaf Clover enhances her multi-attack playstyle, increasing her damage on every third strike.

  • The Bard is the most complex of the classes with a totally different playstyle from the rest thanks to her unique class artifact the Musicians Trick. Her artifact builds up power every time she plays a card, unleashing the stored energy whenever she plays her unique Song cards. Additionally, She utilizes poisons to her advantage, building up damage over the course of a combat.

What makes Into the Dungeon different from other cooperative card-battlers is that all players act in real time. This keeps the pace of the game fast and fun as you simultaneously blast your foes and increase your power. Delve deep into a roguelike deck builder with an easy introduction and a deep challenge to keep you engaged.

Hardcore Mode

Upon completing the game in classic mode you will unlock Hardcore Mode. This mode is designed to challenge experienced rogue-like deck-builder fans. It includes boosted stats for enemies as well as a round timer for some added pressure.

Multiplayer Saves

Pickup where you left off with auto saving in both single player and multiplayer. Simply have the host re-open the lobby and reconnect to jump back into the action.
