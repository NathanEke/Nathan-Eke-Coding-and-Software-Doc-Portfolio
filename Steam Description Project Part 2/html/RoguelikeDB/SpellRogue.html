Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“SpellRogue has been developed openly with the community from day one. Being open and available to our community is a core pillar in how we are developing the game, and we believe that interacting and being in direct dialogue with our players is crucial to making SpellRogue the best game it can be. Allowing players to influence the game is going to continue, and Early Access is a great way to achieve this.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“This is highly dependent on your feedback. We already have a ton of features we hope to implement, but we also want to prioritize what you want to see in the game. At the earliest we might have a full release in 6 months, but it might be much longer.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“First of all, we want to add more variation. Even though the game is already in a fully playable state, we want to add even more replayability: Adding a new Air-themed wizard, adding new boss- and elite enemies, adding more spells, artifacts, relics, potions and mutators. We also have a long list of new features and mechanics we want to explore (some of these might simply be added as separate game modes or modifiers).”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“SpellRogue currently has 3 different wizards, each with their own set of unique Spells and mechanics. The game takes place across 3 Acts filled with distinct enemies, where you are rewarded with all kinds of Artifacts, Spells and Relics. The game also contains a fully featured (and very challenging!) "New Game+" mode, as well as achievements unlocking even more content and loadouts, so there are already a lot of game to sink your teeth into.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“We are still not sure, but we may increase the price slightly. The price itself is going to be comparable to similar titles.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“Our official Discord channel is the primary way we interact with our community, and is where you can discuss the game with us and the community. It’s a great place to post feedback, make suggestions, talk balance and hang out with like minded roguelike enthusiasts. You can also post on the Steam forums which we frequently check, or you can follow SpellRogue on Twitter/X for news, announcements, teasers for the future and goofy GIFs of hundreds of dice rolling all at once.”

About This Game

Dice Gameplay

Manipulate dice in fun and novel ways to control the fickle Mana Dice and bend their fate to your will. Reroll, duplicate, split, flip and enchant with special effects to take command of the Mana Dice's random nature, and use them to cast your mighty spells.



But beware, the foul Voidwalkers are spreading their influence on the once peaceful inhabitants of the world and has made them ravenous. Battle your way through the Deadwoods, across the Scorched Desert and hunt down the source to cleanse the land of the corruption.

Enemies

Engage groups of enemies and prioritize your targets carefully. Learn how each of the different opponents are capable of sabotaging your well laid plans when they interact directly with your spells and Mana Dice. The contagious mushrooms may 'infect' your spells with spreading debuffs, while the undead can curse your Mana Dice to roll poorly. The fiery elementals can even set your spells ablaze, hurting you if they are used.

Wizards and Spells

Meet the band of SpellRogues, each with their own unique elemental theme and special ways of interacting with Mana Dice and mechanics.

  • Azar the Cinder Conjurer: Disassembles Mana Dice and feeds fire spells with smaller Mana Dice to unleash devastating flames.
  • Hazel the Earthen Mage: Can grow or wither Mana Dice and is capable of making nature move to help or hurt.
  • Lapis the Azure Seer: Controls the ebb and flow of Mana Dice and may redirect the forceful surges or rain acid upon foes.

SpellRogue in its current form

  • Play with 3 different wizard archetypes, Water, Earth & Fire.
  • Cast more than ~200 unique spells, each with 2 upgrades.
  • Fight against 50+ different enemies.
  • Loot 80+ different artifacts.
  • Experiment with powerful combinations and make difficult choices.
  • Vanquish the corrupted inhabitants and destroy the Voidwalkers across 3 acts.

Active Development

SpellRogue is currently being developed. You can learn more on the SpellRogue Discord server and get involved in the beta playtesting efforts to help us make the game the very best that it can be.
