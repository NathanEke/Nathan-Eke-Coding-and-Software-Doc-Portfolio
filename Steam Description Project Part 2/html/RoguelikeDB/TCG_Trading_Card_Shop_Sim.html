About This Game

In TCG Trading Card Shop Sim, you take on the role of a TCG shop owner, managing every aspect of your own trading card store.

  • Stock your shelves with the latest trading card packs, exclusive figurines of beloved game characters, and rare collectibles.

  • Customise your shop’s interior to attract more customers and create the ultimate card shop experience.

  • Expand your business as your popularity grows—unlock larger spaces and design unique areas for players to come in and play the trading card game right in your store!

  • Not only can you run a thriving shop, but you can also curate your own personal card collection, hunting down rare cards to show off or trade.

Become the top destination for collectors, players, and fans in TCG Trading Card Shop Sim!
