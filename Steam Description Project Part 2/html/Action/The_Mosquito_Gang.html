About This Game

The Mosquito Gang is a multiplayer game with asymmetric gameplay, where players choose one side in the ongoing war between humans and mosquitoes!

CLEANUP TIME

Piles of dirty dishes? A clogged toilet? Or maybe a dusty carpet? No problem!
All these tasks are easily handled when the Human is on the job.

However, there's a catch: the house is home to a mosquito gang, who will stop at nothing to interfere with the Human’s work.

SIZE DOESN’T MATTER

The mosquito gang's goal is simple — get the Human's precious blood! The hunt for this treasure is full of HUGE risks. The more blood they collect, the more opportunities they gain. Even the smallest warrior can level up by purchasing unique abilities.

The mosquitoes’ main objective is to escape with the loot before the Human catches on.

ALL'S FAIR IN WAR

As the Human completes tasks, they earn money to spend on tools for fighting off pests. The higher the price, the better the gear! There's also a variety of items for other activities to help make the Human's life easier.

The mosquitoes' progression is based on a rogue-lite system, where they can randomly unlock new abilities or modifiers that alter the gameplay. Their currency is the Human's blood.

The great showdown begins!
