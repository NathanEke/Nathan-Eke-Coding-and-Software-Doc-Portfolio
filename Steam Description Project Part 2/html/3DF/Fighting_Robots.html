About This Game

Welcome to the Robot88 tournament, where combat skills are crafted by the robot master.

Create unique combat stances, blocks, and strikes. Perfect them to perfection. Develop your own unique fighting style. Customize your robot by winning battles and unlocking new skins and additional slots for storing created moves.

Participate in ranked battles with other players from around the world to show everyone who is the best of the best.
