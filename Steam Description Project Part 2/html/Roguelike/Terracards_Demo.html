Early Access Game

Get instant access and start playing; get involved with this game as it develops.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“In Early Access, we'll expand Terracards with more plants, animals, and structures. We'll actively seek player feedback through our communities to improve the game by adding ideas given by players. Our goal is a full and diverse game that can be played for at least 500 turns without getting boring, after about 3 months in Early Access, depending on player feedback.”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“About three months. We're planning on releasing the full game on Q2 2024”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“More plants, structures, animals, and essences. You'll be able to get into the late-game through even more alternative paths.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“Terracards in its current Early Access state offers a fully playable game, most planned core features have been implemented. You can already play a run to beginning to end interacting with the tax system, hand, deck, selling, buying new islands, essences that are game changing buffs to your whole farm, fully working production system, 80+ playable cards that will influence your farm in unique ways by producing and/or consuming items and/or resources, and a menu where you can check every card in the game.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“Nope”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“We plan to actively involve our community in the development of Terracards. Players' feedback, ideas, and suggestions will be really important for shaping the game's direction. We'll engage with players through Discord, where we'll share updates and collect feedback to incorporate into game improvements.”

About This Game

Create, Cultivate, Profit!


You've inherited your grandfather's farm, which has been reduced to a lone island amidst a relentless sea. With nothing but a deck of cards and the entrepreneurial spirit, you are tasked to pay off a mounting debt, turning this desolate patch of land into a thriving agrarian empire.

Each card you play represents different elements of your farm - from the crops you sow, the animals you raise, to the structures you build.

Choose, Produce, Profit!


Every few turns choose between unique cards, and buffs

Generate items each turn, sell them for cash, and invest this money to expand your farm, pay off fees, and gradually chip away at the enormous debt.
the challenge is to maximize efficiency and make as much money as possible.
