Coming Soon To Early Access

The developers of this game intend to release as a work in progress, developing with the feedback of players.

Note: Games in Early Access are not complete and may or may not change further. If you are not excited to play this game in its current state, then you should wait to see if the game progresses further in development. Learn more

What the developers have to say:

Why Early Access?

“We are trying to fit into giant (pun intended) footsteps with Industry Giant 4.0 and we are keenly aware that this is a tall (dare we say gigantic) task for a young development team like us.
However, we are working to make fresh and new gaming experience while keeping the old charm. To do that we have already acquired the help of industry (pun not intended) veterans, but we need more. We need you!

By involving you in the process, we want to make sure, that we hit the spots and craft a game for our players, that they love and will play for ages. Developing and refining the game based on your feedback and reactions is important to us!”

Approximately how long will this game be in Early Access?

“Our plan is to stay in Early Access for about a year. As you can see from our roadmap, we have lots of plans, but we try to be flexible to be able to adjust to your needs. We want to achieve great things with you and are willing to listen and enhance the game according to what you think works best.

Some features can be completed sooner, while others are more complex and finding the time to accommodate those features will be essential for our design process.”

How is the full version planned to differ from the Early Access version?

“We see Early Access as a collaborative process, and we are excited to see how your feedback will shape the story of IG 4.0. We know that there is still a lot of work to do. For example, our UI and tutorial are still a little rough around the edges and might be a challenge for new players, but we want to conquer those issues with your feedback. For the full version, we are planning to add more maps and scenarios, new factories and production chains as well as sandbox/custom game and multiplayer modes.”

What is the current state of the Early Access version?

“We’re committed to deliver a high quality gaming experience and are currently focussing on maintaining a good overall performance. We are confident, that you can easily play for 50+hrs if you are playing through our current content, but there is still a lot of things that we want to improve or implement.

During Early Access players can fully play through all available maps, try new strategies and optimize their plans. In addition, we are offering modding from day 1 and while it might not be for everybody, we are looking forward to see and try your creative work.”

Will the game be priced differently during and after Early Access?

“As a token of our appreciation for your contributions during the Early Access, we are offering the game at a lower price until the 1.0 release. We really value your feedback and dedication to making Industry Giant 4.0 the best version of the franchise.”

How are you planning on involving the Community in your development process?

“From our point of view, we are in this together, this means we will be active on social media and Discord to keep you updated and to listen to your thoughts.

We will give our best to provide you with regular updates, fixes and improvements on our official Steam page.

We will also continue to create our devlogs and will provide you with insights on our decisions. As of now we can hardly wait to start this journey with you, and we are looking to pay back the trust you placed in us by being open minded, communicative and as helpful as we can be.”

About This Game


Are you the next Rockefeller, Jobs or Bezos? Put your business acumen to the test as you embark on a journey to economic dominance with Industry Giant 4.0, where you shape an industrial empire from the ground up. This immersive simulation goes beyond mere construction, offering a comprehensive experience in producing goods, building up logistics, industrial building, production planning, and financial management. As a visionary industrial tycoon, you confront the challenges of a dynamic business landscape influenced by global events, environmental regulations, and technological breakthroughs.




Industry Giant 4.0 starts in the 1950’s and embodies the fourth industrial revolution, Industry 4.0, marked by digitization and networked value chains. Experience the progression and evolution to automation and digitization. Engage in strategic decision-making across the value chain, enhancing productivity, flexibility,innovation, and resource efficiency.




In this game, the ripple effects of world events, climate regulations, and economic crises create a thrilling business dynamic, challenging players to adapt and thrive in an ever-changing environment. Are you ready to build, innovate, and conquer the industrial world? Unleash your entrepreneurial spirit and become one of the greatest industrial magnates of all time in Industry Giant 4.0.



25 different industries including mines, farms, manufacturing and more than 70 goods (raw materials, intermediate products, end products) to manage.


Define your own company philosophy at the beginning of the game. These determine which events take place and which options are in play for policies and marketing. In addition, features such as loans and taxes are also influenced.


Utilize data in various tables and charts, monitor the flow of goods, as well as the condition and efficiencies of factories, stores and warehouses to resolve production issues and implement appropriate optimizations.


Various city buildings and many stores in different sizes over several time periods and categories (food, fashion, home decoration/furniture, hardware store, electronics).


Transport goods using trucks, trains and ships. Build roads and railways and set up timetables to specify the quantity of goods to be transported in a certain period of time.


Seasons influence the production of certain raw materials, e.g. food and impact the consumption and purchasing behavior of customers depending on the time of year.

    • OS: Windows 10 (x64)
    • Processor: Intel Core i7-8700k or AMD Ryzen 5 3600
    • Memory: 16 GB RAM
